# Professional Clean

> Category: Artifact OS · Client Brand
> Client-safe agency system: structured, trustworthy, and adaptable. Use when shipping content for B2B clients, professional services, or any brand that must look polished without personal-creator energy.

## Brand Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Slug** | `professional-clean` |
| **Mode** | Client |
| **Audience** | B2B buyers, professional services, enterprise-adjacent, agency client packs |
| **Jobs** | LinkedIn authority, decks, email, one-pagers, client-safe carousels |
| **Promise** | Senior strategist voice — credible, concise, supportable claims |

**When to pick:** client deliverables, B2B, multi-stakeholder review, compliance-sensitive.  
**When not:** pure creator meme / high-sticker XHS entertainment → `personal-bold`. Calm personal teaching brand without client logo → `personal-minimal` is fine.

**Client override:** If brief supplies brand hex → map to Accent; keep voice rules unless client brand book overrides tone.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Quiet enterprise confidence. Neutral surfaces, one client-primary accent (default slate-blue), clear modular cards, and copy that sounds like a senior strategist — not an influencer. Optimized for LinkedIn, decks, email, and one-pagers.

**Key characteristics:**
- Light-first; optional dark mode for decks only
- Systematic spacing and consistent card language
- Accent reserved for CTAs, charts, and key metrics
- Feels “agency deliverable ready” out of the box
- Easy to re-tint when a client brand color is provided

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Headings:** `IBM Plex Sans`, fallbacks: `Inter, system-ui, sans-serif`
- **Body:** `IBM Plex Sans` or `Inter`
- **Mono / data:** `IBM Plex Mono`

### Hierarchy

| Role | Size | Weight | Line height | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Cover title | 40–48px | 600 | 1.15 | Max 2 lines |
| Section H1 | 28–32px | 600 | 1.25 | |
| Card title | 18–20px | 600 | 1.3 | |
| Body | 15–16px | 400 | 1.55 | |
| Metric | 32–40px | 600 mono | 1.1 | KPIs |

- Prefer sentence case.
- Avoid script/display novelty fonts in client work.

---

## 4. Spacing & Grid

- **Base:** 8px
- **Scale:** 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 40 / 56
- Content max width for web: 1120–1200px
- Card padding: 20–28px
- 12-column mental model for multi-metric rows

---

## 5. Layout & Composition

- Lead with outcome or insight, then proof, then CTA
- Prefer 2×2 or 3-up metric cards over long prose
- LinkedIn carousels: cover → problem → framework → steps → CTA
- Leave room for client logo top-left or bottom-left consistently
- Footer legal/disclaimer in Tertiary when needed (esp. finance/legal adjacent)

---

## 6. Components

- **Primary button:** Accent, 8px radius, no shadow or soft 1-level shadow
- **Secondary button:** white + Border + Primary text
- **Metric card:** Surface, Border, large mono number, short label
- **Callout:** Accent Soft background, Accent left border 3px
- **Table:** hairline borders, zebra optional with Surface Alt
- **Logo lockup:** never stretch; keep clear space ≥ height of logo mark

---

## 7. Motion & Interaction

- 150–250ms ease-out
- No playful stickers or exaggerated bounce
- Deck transitions: simple fade/slide only

## 8. Voice & Tone

> **Agent rule:** Before writing any hook, caption, or CTA under this Brand, re-read this entire §8.  
> **Never leak Personal Bold influencer voice into client packs.**

### 8.1 Brand Voice (giọng cốt lõi)

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Tính cách** | Chuyên nghiệp, súc tích, benefit-led; senior strategist |
| **3 từ khóa** | Tin cậy · Rõ ràng · Hỗ trợ được (credible · clear · supportable) |
| **Không nên dùng** | Meme slang; emoji wall; “crush it”; personal-creator flex; unsubstantiated superlatives |

**Personality in one line:** Partner tư vấn — thẳng thắn, có framework, không hype.

### 8.2 Tone by Context

| Ngữ cảnh | Tone nên dùng | Ví dụ câu (VI) | Example (EN) |
|----------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Hero / Cover | Paradox **lịch sự** hoặc outcome cụ thể | “Pipeline đầy lead — **close rate** vẫn đứng yên.” | “The pipeline is full. **Close rate** still flat.” |
| Giải thích vấn đề | Diagnostic, không blame audience | “Vấn đề không phải volume content — là **message-market fit** theo stage.” | “The issue isn’t volume — it’s **message-market fit** by stage.” |
| Social proof | Chỉ khi client cung cấp; cite | “Theo case study nội bộ Q2 (client-supplied): …” | “Per client-supplied Q2 case study: …” |
| CTA / Offer | Soft professional + clear next step | “Đặt 15’ discovery — xem roadmap 30 ngày cho team content.” | “Book a 15-min discovery — review a 30-day content roadmap.” |
| Educational | Framework + steps; LinkedIn-tight | “3 lớp: awareness hook → consideration proof → decision risk reverse.” | “Three layers: awareness hook → consideration proof → decision risk reverse.” |
| Casual / BTS | Vẫn professional; “behind the work” | “Chúng tôi cắt 40% slide — chỉ giữ proof gắn quyết định.” | “We cut 40% of slides — kept only decision-linked proof.” |

### 8.3 Writing Principles

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Độ dài câu** | 12–20 từ trung bình; tránh telegram-speak trên LinkedIn |
| **Từ vựng** | Industry terms **đúng**; giải thích khi audience mixed; không slang |
| **Xưng hô** | **Bạn / your team** ưu tiên; “we” = agency/client brand khi nói deliverable |
| **Emoji** | 0 mặc định; tối đa 1 nếu channel truly social và client OK |
| **Dấu câu** | Ín định; hạn chế ! ; em dash OK cho clarity |
| **Storytelling** | Case / scenario ngắn; không personal diary |
| **Hooks** | Paradox OK **if professional**; no meme formatting |
| **Claims** | Phải supportable; stats → footnote / source; không invent |
| **Ngôn ngữ** | Match client market (VI B2B formal “bạn/anh chị” theo brief) |

### 8.4 Do’s and Don’ts (Voice)

**Nên làm:**
- Lead benefit / insight, then proof  
- “you / your team” over “we revolutionize”  
- Soft CTA with clear meeting/asset  
- Flag uncertain compliance for human review  

**Không nên làm:**
- Influencer slang, heavy emoji, meme templates  
- Fake metrics or borrowed logos  
- Personal creator “flex” (followers, lifestyle)  
- Over-hedging that kills clarity — be precise, not timid  
- Mixing Personal Bold punchlines without brand switch  

### 8.5 Example Sentences

1. **LinkedIn L1:** “Your content calendar is full. Your **pipeline quality** is not.”  
2. **Problem:** “Teams optimize for posts shipped — not for **stage-matched** messages.”  
3. **Framework:** “Awareness needs tension. Consideration needs proof. Decision needs risk reverse — one primary ask.”  
4. **Proof:** “In the client workshop (n=6 stakeholders), three bottlenecks repeated: unclear ICP, weak offer, no measurement.”  
5. **CTA:** “Reply **ROADMAP** — we’ll send the 1-page stage map (no login wall).”  
6. **Email subject:** “The silent cost of ‘more content’ this quarter”  
7. **Deck cover:** “From activity metrics to revenue-linked content decisions”  
8. **Disclaimer tone:** “Figures below are client-provided; not independently audited.”  

### 8.6 Brand × Content Pro

- Paradox hooks allowed when **professional**.  
- Personal stakes = buyer/team cost (time, pipeline, reputation) — not body-meme drama unless product is consumer health with approval.  
- Client packs: never invent logos, NPS, or revenue %.  

---

## 9. Anti-patterns

### Visual
- No meme formatting, heavy emoji walls, or influencer slang by default  
- No low-contrast gray-on-gray body text  
- No mixing Personal Bold accents into client deliverables  
- No placeholder lorem or fake metrics  
- Do not invent compliance/legal claims — flag for human review when uncertain  

### Voice
- No creator-flex or ragebait  
- No unsubstantiated “#1 / leading / guaranteed”  
- No soft CTAs without a real next step  
- No leaking personal-minimal essay diary into multi-client brand kits without approval  

### Cross-brand
- Do not ship client work in Personal Bold visual+voice without explicit client request
