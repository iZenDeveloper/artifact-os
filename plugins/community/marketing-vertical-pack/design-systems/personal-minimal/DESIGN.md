# Personal Minimal

> Category: Artifact OS · Personal Brand
> Clean personal brand for creators who want calm authority, high readability, and zero visual noise. Default for long-form, carousels, and client-safe personal content.

## Brand Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Slug** | `personal-minimal` |
| **Mode** | Personal |
| **Audience** | Founders, consultants, educators, creators who teach more than they “hype” |
| **Jobs** | LinkedIn, Threads, email, XHS/carousels when calm trust beats spectacle |
| **Promise** | Clear thinking, readable type, hooks that punch **without** shouty branding |

**When to pick this brand:** personal authority, thoughtful storytelling, bilingual VI/EN, Content Pro packs that should feel essay-grade not meme-grade.

**When not to:** pure entertainment XHS/TikTok that needs sticker energy → use `personal-bold`. Strict client agency decks → use `professional-clean`.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

A light, breathable canvas with near-black type and a single restrained accent. Feels like a well-typeset essay or a quiet portfolio — not a startup landing page. Prefer generous whitespace, soft borders, and one clear hierarchy at a time.

**Key characteristics:**
- Light-mode native: warm off-white surface, charcoal text
- One accent only (ink blue) for CTAs and emphasis
- Large readable type; no decorative gradients or glassmorphism
- Cards with subtle hairline borders, not heavy shadows
- Works for LinkedIn carousels, Threads posts, email headers, and simple landing sections

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Surfaces
- **Canvas** (`#FAFAF8`): Page / slide background — warm paper white
- **Surface** (`#FFFFFF`): Cards, panels, elevated blocks
- **Surface Muted** (`#F2F1EE`): Secondary blocks, quote backgrounds
- **Border** (`#E6E4DF`): Hairline dividers and card edges

### Text
- **Primary Text** (`#1A1A1A`): Headlines and body
- **Secondary Text** (`#5C5C5C`): Supporting copy, captions
- **Tertiary Text** (`#8A8A8A`): Metadata, timestamps, watermarks

### Brand & Status
- **Accent** (`#1F4B99`): Links, primary CTA, key highlight
- **Accent Soft** (`#E8EEF8`): Soft chip / tag background
- **Success** (`#1F7A4C`)
- **Warning** (`#B36B00`)
- **Danger** (`#B42318`)

**Usage rules:**
- Favor Accent for one CTA per frame only.
- Never use pure black (`#000`) or pure neon colors.
- Keep contrast WCAG AA for body text on Canvas/Surface.

---

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Display / Headings:** `Source Serif 4`, fallbacks: `Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif`
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, fallbacks: `system-ui, -apple-system, Segoe UI, sans-serif`
- **Mono (optional):** `JetBrains Mono`, `ui-monospace, Menlo, monospace`

### Hierarchy

| Role | Family | Size | Weight | Line height | Notes |
|------|--------|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Display | Source Serif 4 | 40–48px | 600 | 1.15 | Cover titles |
| H1 | Source Serif 4 | 32px | 600 | 1.2 | Section titles |
| H2 | Inter | 22–24px | 600 | 1.3 | Card titles |
| Body | Inter | 16–18px | 400 | 1.55 | Default copy |
| Caption | Inter | 13–14px | 500 | 1.4 | Meta / labels |

- Prefer sentence case for body; title case only for short cover lines.
- Max ~2 type sizes visible on a single social card besides the watermark.

---

## 4. Spacing & Grid

- **Base unit:** 8px
- **Scale:** 4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64
- Card internal padding: 24–32px
- Section gap: 32–48px
- Align content to a simple single-column or 12-column loose grid; avoid dense dashboards.

---

## 5. Layout & Composition

- Hierarchy: hook → one idea → proof/example → soft CTA
- One primary message per card / frame
- Prefer left-aligned text; center only on cover slides
- Safe margins ≥ 48px on social export sizes (1080×1350, 1080×1440)
- Watermark / handle: bottom-right, Tertiary Text, 12–13px

---

## 6. Components

- **Primary button:** Accent fill, white text, 10–12px radius, no heavy shadow
- **Secondary button:** Border only (`Border`), Primary Text
- **Chip / tag:** Accent Soft background, Accent text
- **Card:** Surface bg, 1px Border, 12–16px radius
- **Quote block:** Surface Muted left border 3px Accent
- **Divider:** 1px Border, never double lines

---

## 7. Motion & Interaction

- Transitions 150–200ms, ease-out only
- No bounce, no parallax, no autoplay video by default
- Hover: slight opacity or border darkening — not scale transforms

## 8. Voice & Tone

> **Agent rule:** Before writing any hook, caption, or CTA under this Brand, re-read this entire §8. Visual tokens without voice = **FAIL**.

### 8.1 Brand Voice (giọng cốt lõi)

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Tính cách** | Thân thiện nhưng chuyên nghiệp; calm authority; teacher-friend, not cheerleader |
| **3 từ khóa** | Rõ · Đáng tin · Có chiều sâu (clear · trustworthy · deep) |
| **Không nên dùng** | Hype rỗng; slang quá đà; corporate buzzword; “crush it / game-changing / revolutionary” |

**Personality in one line:** Người viết như mentor đã từng fail — nói thẳng, không la hét.

### 8.2 Tone by Context

| Ngữ cảnh | Tone nên dùng | Ví dụ câu (VI) | Example (EN) |
|----------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Hero / Cover hook | Tĩnh nhưng căng — paradox hoặc cost, không la | “3 trái tim… và 1 trái **gần như chết** mỗi khi bơi.” | “Three hearts — and one **almost dies** every time it swims.” |
| Giải thích vấn đề | Đồng cảm + cụ thể; không moralize | “Bạn không lười. Bạn đang trả **hóa đơn ẩn** của quy trình sai.” | “You’re not lazy. You’re paying the **hidden bill** of a broken process.” |
| Social proof | Cụ thể, khiêm tốn; không bịa % | “3 founder B2B SaaS VN dùng checklist này sau buổi office hour.” | “Three B2B SaaS founders used this checklist after office hours.” |
| CTA / Offer | Rõ deliverable + keyword; soft, không ép | “Comment **GUIDE** — nhận checklist 5 bước (PDF).” | “Comment **GUIDE** — get the 5-step checklist (PDF).” |
| Educational | Story beat + portable lesson | “Nature chấp nhận bơi kém… để sống sót môi trường **duy nhất** quan trọng.” | “Nature accepts being bad at swimming — to survive the **only** environment that matters.” |
| Casual / BTS | Ấm, ngắn, 1 insight; vẫn không meme-spam | “Draft #4 mới đủ mạnh. Hook đầu quá ‘fact Wikipedia’.” | “Draft #4 finally worked. V1 was pure Wikipedia title.” |

### 8.3 Writing Principles

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Độ dài câu** | Trung bình 8–16 từ; 1 câu dài / đoạn tối đa để “xoắn” insight |
| **Từ vựng** | Ưu tiên từ đơn giản + 1–2 thuật ngữ khi cần authority; giải thích thuật ngữ ngay |
| **Xưng hô** | **Bạn** / **you** là mặc định; “chúng tôi” chỉ khi nói về brand/team thật |
| **Emoji** | 0–1 / post; không wall emoji; XHS cho phép 1–2 nếu native |
| **Dấu câu** | Em dash / ellipsis cho open loop; hạn chế !!! |
| **Storytelling** | **Có** — scene + turn + portable lesson (Content Pro); không encyclopedia |
| **Hooks** | Flat fact **cấm**; fact + cost/paradox/almost-loss + open loop |
| **Ngôn ngữ** | VI hoặc EN (hoặc mix có chủ đích); **cùng tone** ở cả hai |
| **Clean ≠ weak** | Minimal visual **vẫn** được paradox / personal stakes |

### 8.4 Do’s and Don’ts (Voice)

**Nên làm:**
- Mở bằng cost / paradox / almost-loss gắn **bạn / body / home** khi cần drama  
- CTA = keyword **+** deliverable cụ thể  
- Proof thật hoặc labeled assumption; portable lesson có răng  
- Câu ngắn, động từ cụ thể  

**Không nên làm:**
- “Game-changing / revolutionary / crush it / unlock your potential”  
- Fake scarcity, fake %, fake logo  
- Soft CTA mơ hồ: “Learn more” / “Follow for more” không offer  
- Fact-list title không stakes  
- Influencer shout khi brand này đang active  

### 8.5 Example Sentences (agent học theo)

1. **Hook:** “Bạn không thiếu ý tưởng — bạn đang **mất 40 phút** mỗi lần mở doc trắng.”  
2. **Problem:** “Content ‘đủ hay’ vẫn chết vì **dòng đầu** không bắt buộc phải đọc tiếp.”  
3. **Insight:** “Clean design không cứu được hook yếu. Nó chỉ làm sự nhạt **trông** chuyên nghiệp hơn.”  
4. **Proof (honest):** “Trong 12 pack gần nhất, pack ≥9.0 đều có **personal stakes** ở giây 1 — không chỉ spectacle.”  
5. **CTA:** “Comment **SPINE** — gửi khung Core → Proof → Insight → Offer.”  
6. **LinkedIn L1:** “The deck looked premium. The pipeline still leaked **two weeks** of founder time.”  
7. **Email subject:** “Your CTA fails before the click — and it’s not the button color.”  
8. **BTS:** “V1: fact list. V3: almost-loss + you. Giữ V3.”  

### 8.6 Brand × Content Pro

- Content Pro drama **được phép và được khuyến khích** dưới Personal Minimal.  
- Visual stay quiet; **copy carries the punch**.

## 9. Anti-patterns

### Visual
- No neon gradients, glass blur, or 3D mockups unless the brief demands product UI  
- No more than one accent color family per artifact  
- No wall of text — break into cards or bullets  
- No stock handshakes / laptops unless brand-specific  
- Do not mix serif with decorative script fonts  

### Voice
- No hype spam or meme voice under this brand  
- No flat fact hooks  
- No vague CTAs without offer  
- No inventing metrics or social proof  
- Do not ship “pretty HTML, weak line 1”  

### Cross-brand
- Do not pull Personal Bold sticker energy or Professional Clean pure-corporate hedging into the same artifact without an explicit brand switch
