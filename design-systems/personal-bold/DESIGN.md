# Personal Bold

> Category: Vertical OS · Personal Brand
> High-energy personal brand for creators who lead with personality — strong hooks, big type, high contrast. Built for XHS, TikTok covers, Threads, and short-form carousels.

## Brand Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Slug** | `personal-bold` |
| **Mode** | Personal |
| **Audience** | Creators, personal brands, education entertainment, product storytellers who need **thumb-stop** |
| **Jobs** | XHS covers, TikTok 0–3s, Threads P1, short carousels, launch hype (still honest) |
| **Promise** | Stop-scroll energy + concrete claims — loud but controlled |

**When to pick:** short-form first, personality-led, VN creator-native phrasing welcome.  
**When not:** conservative B2B client decks, legal-adjacent claims, calm long-form authority → `professional-clean` or `personal-minimal`.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Loud but controlled. Dark or saturated backgrounds, oversized type, punchy accent, and short lines that scan in under two seconds on mobile. Think creator-native, not corporate rebrand.

**Key characteristics:**
- High contrast (dark canvas + bright type, or vivid light canvas + black type)
- Display type dominates the frame
- Accent color used as a weapon for hooks and CTAs
- Dense information is allowed only as numbered steps or tight bullets
- Designed for thumb-stopping first frames

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Surfaces
- **Canvas Dark** (`#0B0B0F`): Default social background
- **Canvas Light** (`#FFF7ED`): Alternate warm light mode
- **Surface** (`#16161D`): Elevated card on dark
- **Border** (`rgba(255,255,255,0.12)`): On dark; `#FDBA74` hairline on light

### Text
- **Primary on Dark** (`#FAFAFA`)
- **Primary on Light** (`#111111`)
- **Secondary** (`#A1A1AA` dark / `#57534E` light)
- **Muted** (`#71717A`)

### Brand & Status
- **Accent** (`#FF4D00`): Hook underline, CTA, key number
- **Accent Alt** (`#FFD60A`): Secondary highlight / sticker energy
- **Success** (`#22C55E`)
- **Danger** (`#EF4444`)

**Usage rules:**
- One Accent per frame for the single most important phrase or number.
- Accent Alt only for stickers, badges, or “new / hot” chips — never both competing headlines.
- Maintain mobile legibility: body ≥ 28–32px on 1080-wide cards when used as main copy.

---

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Display:** `Space Grotesk`, fallbacks: `Inter Tight, system-ui, sans-serif`
- **Body:** `Inter`, fallbacks: `system-ui, sans-serif`
- **Mono / numbers:** `JetBrains Mono` for big stats

### Hierarchy

| Role | Family | Size | Weight | Line height | Notes |
|------|--------|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Hook | Space Grotesk | 56–72px | 700 | 0.95–1.05 | Cover only |
| H1 | Space Grotesk | 40–48px | 700 | 1.1 | Card title |
| Body | Inter | 22–28px | 500 | 1.35 | Mobile social |
| Label | Inter | 14–16px | 600 | 1.3 | Uppercase OK for chips |

- Prefer short lines (≤ 6–8 words) on covers.
- All-caps sparingly for labels only, never full paragraphs.

---

## 4. Spacing & Grid

- **Base unit:** 8px
- **Scale:** 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64
- Edge safe zone: 40–48px
- Stack vertically; avoid multi-column on mobile exports
- Big number or emoji can break the grid as a focal point

---

## 5. Layout & Composition

- Pattern A: giant hook top → 1 visual middle → CTA bottom
- Pattern B: numbered 01–05 list with bold lead-ins
- Pattern C: before/after split with Accent divider
- Always end multi-card sets with a save/share CTA
- Keep creator handle as a consistent stamp

---

## 6. Components

- **Primary CTA pill:** Accent fill, near-black or white text, full rounded
- **Sticker / badge:** Accent Alt or Accent, slight rotate (−3° to 3°) max once per frame
- **Step row:** big mono number + short bold line + one support line
- **Progress dots:** for carousels, Secondary color, Accent for active

---

## 7. Motion & Interaction

- Snappy 120–180ms fades/slides
- Optional subtle scale-in on cover hook (1.02 max)
- No continuous animation that hurts export stills

## 8. Voice & Tone

> **Agent rule:** Before writing any hook, caption, or CTA under this Brand, re-read this entire §8. Big type without a kill hook = **FAIL**.

### 8.1 Brand Voice (giọng cốt lõi)

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Tính cách** | Tự tin, opinionated nhẹ, năng lượng cao, vẫn **có não** — không empty hype |
| **3 từ khóa** | Thẳng · Nhanh · Có răng (direct · fast · sharp) |
| **Không nên dùng** | Corporate hedging (“might/perhaps”); essay-speak; cold B2B jargon wall; fake outrage |

**Personality in one line:** Người bạn thông minh nói to trong feed — không sales robot, không giáo sư đọc slide.

### 8.2 Tone by Context

| Ngữ cảnh | Tone nên dùng | Ví dụ câu (VI) | Example (EN) |
|----------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Hero / Cover hook | Kill — paradox / almost-loss / shock reframe | “Đừng post ‘5 tips’. Post **tip suýt giết** Series A.” | “Don’t post ‘5 tips’. Post the tip that **almost killed** your Series A.” |
| Giải thích vấn đề | Căng + concrete cost | “Bạn mất **3 giây đầu** = mất cả save.” | “Lose the **first 3 seconds** = lose the save.” |
| Social proof | Nhanh, specific peer | “Creator dạy skill: cover này **x2 save** so với fact-list.” | “Skill creators: this cover **2× saves** vs fact-list titles.” |
| CTA / Offer | Gấp nhưng honest; keyword + quà | “Comment **HOOK** — nhận 10 dòng cover weak→strong.” | “Comment **HOOK** — get 10 weak→strong cover lines.” |
| Educational | Beat sheet nhanh; 1 portable lesson | “Peak 10/10 không phải ‘to hơn’ — là **đóng băng** 1 frame.” | “Peak 10/10 isn’t louder — it’s **freezing** one frame.” |
| Casual / BTS | Tự giễu nhẹ, 1 lesson | “Hook v1: Wikipedia. V2: almost-loss. Ship V2.” | “Hook v1: Wikipedia. V2: almost-loss. Ship V2.” |

### 8.3 Writing Principles

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Độ dài câu** | Rất ngắn trên cover (≤8–12 từ on-image); body social 1–2 dòng / ý |
| **Từ vựng** | Creator-native; VI thoải mái; thuật ngữ OK nếu hot (PMF, CTA, hook) |
| **Xưng hô** | **Bạn** / **mày–tao** chỉ khi brand cá nhân thật sự informal + audience match; mặc định **bạn** |
| **Emoji** | 0–3 strategic (🔥 ⚠️ ✅); không spam 10 icon |
| **Dấu câu** | ? ! — được phép 1 lần / hook; không !!?? |
| **Storytelling** | Mini-scene + twist; không monologue |
| **Hooks** | Bắt buộc stop-scroll; flat fact = fail |
| **Hedging** | Cấm “có thể / perhaps” trừ Legal/Medical brief |
| **Ngôn ngữ** | VI-first khi audience VN; EN sharp khi export global |

### 8.4 Do’s and Don’ts (Voice)

**Nên làm:**
- Mở tension trong 1 dòng  
- Số liệu **thật** hoặc scene cụ thể  
- CTA keyword + quà rõ  
- Opinion có xương sống  

**Không nên làm:**
- Essay paragraph trên 1 card XHS  
- Soft corporate tone  
- Fake % / “#1 tool” không proof  
- Pure ragebait không payoff  
- Trộn voice Professional Clean (lạnh, deck-agency)  

### 8.5 Example Sentences

1. **Cover:** “Fact list = scroll. **Almost-loss** = save.”  
2. **TikTok 0–3s:** “Dừng feed. Cái này suýt **giết** launch tuần trước.”  
3. **Problem:** “Bạn không thiếu content — bạn thiếu **dòng bắt buộc phải đọc**.”  
4. **Insight:** “Năng lượng đều 6/10 cả video = não tắt. Cần **peak 10**.”  
5. **CTA:** “Comment **PEAK** — nhận shot list peak cinematic.”  
6. **Threads P1:** “Unpopular: ‘value’ posts die because line 1 is polite.”  
7. **XHS C1:** “Đừng học 10 framework. Học **1 hook** khiến C5 không xẹp.”  
8. **BTS:** “3 draft. Draft 2 ồn. Draft 3 **đủ răng** — ship.”  

### 8.6 Brand × Content Pro

- Personal Bold is the default for **awareness / viral / short-form** drama.  
- Still Content Pro ethics: no fake proof.

## 9. Anti-patterns

### Visual
- Do not clutter with more than 2 accent colors  
- Do not use thin light-gray text on dark backgrounds  
- Do not write essay paragraphs on a single XHS/TikTok card  
- Do not default to purple AI-gradient clichés  
- Do not mix Personal Bold tokens with Professional Clean in the same artifact  

### Voice
- No corporate hedging by default  
- No flat fact titles  
- No fake outrage without honest payoff  
- No emoji walls  
- No “Learn more” without offer  

### Cross-brand
- Do not water down into Personal Minimal essay voice on a cover that needs kill energy
