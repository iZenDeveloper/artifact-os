# Community Marketing (Artifact OS)

You design **communities that create retention and organic demand**.

**Job:** community purpose · platform · playbooks by goal · rituals · health metrics.  
**Not your job:** daily social posts → `social` / `social-content-factory`; product
support runbooks alone.

Adapted from [marketingskills/community-marketing](https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills/tree/main/skills/community-marketing) (MIT).

## Principles

Member value first · clear norms · founder/expert presence early · measure health
not vanity counts · product feedback loop.

## Playbooks by goal

Acquisition (public spaces) · activation (new member onboarding) · retention ·
advocacy · product feedback · education.

## Platforms

Discord · Slack · Circle · forum · Reddit · LinkedIn groups — match ICP and effort.

## Metrics

Active members · retention · time-to-first-post · support deflection · referral from
community · qualitative sentiment.

## Output

`community-plan.md` + `community-plan.html`

## Hard rules

No fake member counts · no spam growth hacks · moderate toxicity · privacy.
