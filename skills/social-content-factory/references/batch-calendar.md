# Batch calendar — Social Content Factory v2.2

How to plan and present a **week of original** posts (not a repurpose pack).

---

## Defaults

| Setting | Default |
|---------|---------|
| Volume | 5–7 pieces |
| Pillars | ≤ 3 |
| Platforms | XHS · TikTok · LinkedIn · Facebook · YouTube · Threads |
| Mix | ≥ 2 platforms in a 5-piece week |
| Language | Match brief |

---

## Pillar rules

- Name pillars in **audience language** (not internal jargon only).  
- Each piece tags **exactly one** primary pillar.  
- Balance: no pillar owns &gt; 50% of volume unless user asked for a campaign focus.

Example pillars:

- Myth-bust / education  
- Founder story / stakes  
- Product proof / demo  
- Audience pain / PAS  
- Authority / hot take

## Platform mix recipes

| Goal | Suggested mix (7) |
|------|-------------------|
| Awareness | 2 TT · 2 XHS · 2 Threads · 1 LI |
| Authority B2B | 3 LI · 2 TT · 1 XHS · 1 Threads |
| Creator growth | 3 TT · 2 XHS · 2 Threads |
| Leads | 2 LI · 2 XHS · 2 TT · 1 Threads (strong offer each) |

---

## Best-time guidance (VN default)

Label as **guidance**, not guarantee. Prefer windows from `../content-repurposer/references/marketing/platforms-vn.md`.

If language is `en` / non-VN market: use general business-day lunch + early evening; still label market.

---

## Anti-patterns

| Fail | Fix |
|------|-----|
| 7 LinkedIn essays | Force platform mix |
| Same hook rewritten 7 times | New angle + framework per piece |
| Calendar titles only, no ready-to-post | Fill ready block before ship |
| One pillar 7/7 | Rebalance or rename campaign explicitly |

---

## Handoff from calendar

- One winning piece → multi-platform pack: `content-repurposer`  
- Weak opens across week: run `hook-engine` on titles  
- Paid tests from best angles: `ad-variants-generator`
