## Universal recipe

```
[Unexpected fact or belief flip]
+ [cost / paradox / almost-loss]
+ [YOU / body / home when drama needs memory]
+ [open loop — partial payoff only]
```

**Fail modes**

| Mode | Symptom | Fix |
|------|---------|-----|
| Flat fact | Wikipedia title | Add cost or almost-loss |
| Spectacle only | Cool system, no you | Land bill on body/home/identity |
| Soft verbs | “interesting”, “amazing” | Concrete cost verb (kill, burn, stall, leak) |
| Closed loop | Full answer in line 1 | Withhold mechanism until body |
| Wrong funnel | Hard sell cold | Awareness → curiosity; decision → risk reverse |
| Length miss | Essay on cover | Cut to surface budget |

## Surface constraints

### XHS cover (on-image)

- **Budget:** ~8–12 words (language-dependent; shorter wins)  
- **Job:** stop-scroll + open loop; no full insight  
- **Strong:** paradox or almost-loss + *you*  
- **Weak:** tip list title, soft “must-know”

### TikTok 0–3s

- **Budget:** 1–2 short spoken lines + matching on-screen  
- **Job:** energy **10**; filmable (agent can imagine a frame)  
- **Strong:** kill hook then setup gesture  
- **Weak:** “Hey guys today we talk about…”

### LinkedIn L1

- **Budget:** 1 punchy line (can be longer than XHS; still not a paragraph)  
- **Job:** professional stakes; invite the rest  
- **Strong:** paradox in work/life cost  
- **Weak:** “I’m excited to share…” / pure lecture thesis

### Threads / X P1

- **Budget:** platform short post  
- **Job:** strongest chain opener; earn post 2  
- **Strong:** personal bill + open loop  
- **Weak:** “Thread 🧵 about X” with no stakes

### Email subjects

- Ship **3–4** options; **≥1** paradox / almost-loss  
- Avoid spammy ALL CAPS / fake Re:  
- Preview text can continue the loop (optional note)

### Ad headlines

- Respect platform length (Meta primary ~125 chars context; headlines shorter)  
- Tag **angle**; one claim per line  
- No invented % or “#1” without user proof

## Weak → Strong bank (templates)

Replace `{}` with source-specific nouns. Do not ship templates raw.

| Weak | Strong template |
|------|-----------------|
| “{Topic} has {N} {facts}” | “{N} {parts} — and one **almost fails** every time you {action}” |
| “How to {goal}” | “The {goal} playbook that **costs** you {scarce resource} first” |
| “Why {thing} matters” | “{Thing} only matters when **{you-stakes}** is already on fire” |
| “{N} tips for {role}” | “Tip #{k} almost **ended** our {outcome} — here’s the save” |
| “Introducing {product}” | “We shipped {product} to **{honest cold metric}** — then this line fixed the cover” |
